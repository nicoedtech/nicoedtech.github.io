Nico keeps students engaged, allowing teachers to utilize  the benefits of technology in the classroom without having to worry about digital distraction.

Getting the attention of students and keeping them engaged has always been the primary challenge for classroom teachers. And that challenge has become more difficult to overcome as schools shift to using hardware devices to deliver instruction. Why? Because the device, which has the potential to improve instruction, has become a new distraction for students as they stare into cyberspace, surf the net, or hide behind the screen, avoiding contact with the teacher. The solution? Nico Gamification Engagement System!

Earn Nico Coins

Students earn Nico Coins for being on-task and engaged throughout a given lesson. 

Track Nico Stats

Students, Teachers and Principals can view Nico Stats in the Nico Dashboard to track engagement level and monitor progress.

Games and Rewards

Teachers can assist students in earning more Nico Coins while simultaneously engaging them in the lesson.

The digital age provides teachers with convenient access to numerous educational software applications and efficient hardware systems, but it also brings frustrating challenges in the area of student engagement. In today’s classroom, numerous digital distractions compete for students’ attention, derailing even the most expertly delivered lesson. When I saw teachers who were once excited about digital resources turning their backs on technology in frustration, I knew there had to be a solution. So I created Nico! Nico gives teachers the power to easily eliminate digital distraction, while simultaneously rewarding students for staying engaged in any lesson with fun, motivational games.
